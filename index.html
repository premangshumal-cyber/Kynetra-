About Kynetra

Where rigor meets readability

Kynetra started in 2021 as a small newsletter written by two remote-sensing researchers frustrated with shallow tech coverage. Today it's a full publication spanning artificial intelligence, hydrology, geospatial science and software engineering — written by practitioners, not generalists.

Every article is fact-checked, peer-reviewed by a subject specialist, and edited for clarity before it reaches your feed. We believe complex ideas deserve careful explanation, not just a catchy headline.
